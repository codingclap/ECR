Thriving Eco City-Regions

Towards green, inclusive, and resilient cities and city regions

Learn More

What we do

It is imperative that we expand the way we think and plan our cities and city regions. Rainmatter Foundation and WRI India’s partnership to further the ‘Thriving Eco City initiative supports this transition of urban areas into green, inclusive and resilient city-regions.

Our mission

To nudge the business-as-usual approach and create pathways that place cities on the trajectory of more environmentally sustainable and socially equitable development.

Our Theory Of Change

To support five city-regions along the continuum of ecological sustainability and economic growth with a strong focus on equity. We aim to develop empirically driven spatial and statistical analysis, create multi-stakeholder forums for dialogue and collaborative action, strengthen local capacity and capability and empower local stakeholders to be change makers through our technical support.
